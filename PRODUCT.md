# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

The primary users are Que Media's founders, strategists, researchers, and sales team. They use the product during focused desk work to identify a small number of high-potential Ottawa businesses, understand each one deeply, and prepare a confident manual outreach conversation. Secondary users are Que Media account and strategy team members who revisit the same intelligence after a prospect responds or becomes a client.

The core job is to turn fragmented public business information into a verified, explainable intelligence package with a clear next action and follow-up date.

## Product Purpose

Que Media Intelligence autonomously discovers, enriches, audits, compares, and prioritizes Ottawa businesses without contacting them. It prepares client-ready reports, timely reasons to reach out, tailored content strategies, and channel-specific outreach drafts for human review.

Success means the team spends less time assembling research, understands each business better before the first conversation, selects leads by expected return on effort, and earns more positive responses through relevance instead of volume.

## Positioning

Know the business before you speak: every score, recommendation, and message is grounded in visible evidence and shaped for Que Media's content-led growth strengths.

## Brand Personality

Precise, perceptive, and human. The product should feel calm under heavy information, locally grounded in Ottawa, direct without being blunt, and premium through craft rather than luxury styling. Copy should be conversational, specific, and outcome-focused. It should connect marketing observations to customer acquisition instead of celebrating vanity metrics.

## Anti-references

This is not a CRM, bulk prospecting database, outreach sequencer, purchased lead list, or automatic email system. It must not resemble a generic sales dashboard, an engagement-bait analytics toy, or a black-box AI scorecard. Avoid generic praise, intrusive personal profiling, unverified claims, automatic sending, public pricing, decorative social-logo clouds, crypto-style glow, and UI decoration that does not help a decision.

## Design Principles

1. Evidence before confidence. Every consequential claim exposes its source, observation date, freshness, and confidence.
2. Quality before volume. The default workflow narrows the field and deepens understanding instead of maximizing lead count.
3. Context before contact. No outreach asset appears complete until fit, pain, timing, personality, and likely objections are understood.
4. Que Media fit over generic need. Prioritization rewards businesses suited to filmable stories, educational content, entertaining short-form video, community relevance, and measurable growth.
5. Humans own the relationship. Agents research and prepare; a Que Media team member reviews, edits, and sends every interaction.
6. Empty means empty. The product never ships fabricated businesses, observations, metrics, evidence, activity, or outreach drafts. Until verified information exists, the interface explains what is missing and how real records can enter the workspace.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support full keyboard operation, visible focus, semantic landmarks and controls, screen reader-friendly evidence and charts, non-color status cues, 44px touch targets, 200 percent zoom and reflow, and reduced-motion preferences. Dense views must preserve readable type and offer progressive disclosure on smaller screens.

Public data must be handled with professional restraint. Decision-maker research is limited to publicly available, outreach-relevant information, with provenance and clear controls for correction or removal.

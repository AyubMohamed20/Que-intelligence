# Ottawa wellness and specialty-service lead dossier

Research captured July 20, 2026. These are seven real, independent Ottawa-area businesses researched to outreach-ready depth for Que Media. Contacts and profiles are public and cited; no email address was inferred. Social links establish the official footprint only. Because several social feeds could not be read reliably without login, this dossier makes no unsupported follower, engagement, or posting-frequency claims.

The strict machine-readable record is in `research/wellness-leads.json`. It includes every source, observation, score dimension, concept, and outreach field.

## Scoring method

- **Content Fit /100:** visual demonstrations 25, expert cast 20, educational depth 20, recurring stories 20, production feasibility 15.
- **Opportunity /100:** timing 25, gap clarity 25, commercial capacity 25, reachability 15, Ottawa relevance 10.
- **Response /100:** Que Media fit 30, personalization strength 30, Why Now strength 20, contactability 20.
- Scores are analyst estimates supported by the cited evidence. They are prioritization aids, not observed performance metrics.
- A `null` Why Now means no credible last-30-day business-change signal was found. An undated page observed live is labelled as such, not treated as a recent publication.

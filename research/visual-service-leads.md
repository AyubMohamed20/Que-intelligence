# Verified Ottawa Visual-Service Leads

Research completed: **2026-07-20 (America/Toronto)**  
Fresh-signal window: **2026-06-20 through 2026-07-20**  
Scope: six independent Ottawa-area businesses outside hospitality and wellness, selected for high-ticket or high-volume services, strong visual inventory, local decision-maker access, and a credible role for Que Media's entertaining and educational short-form video.

## Verification and scoring rules

- A contact is included only when it is published by the business or corroborated by two public business directories. No email pattern was guessed.
- A social account is called official only when linked from the business's website, its official Linktree, or a clearly matching company profile that links back to the same business.
- No follower, engagement, review, revenue, or audience-size estimate is used. Platform pages that could not be inspected without login were not treated as evidence.
- “No 30-day signal verified” means the search found no event with a visible publication or occurrence date inside the window. It does not mean the business is inactive.
- Scores are decision-support heuristics, not measured probabilities. **Content Fit** is the sum of visual inventory (25), teachable expertise (25), people/story potential (20), Ottawa relevance (15), and repeatable formats (15). **Opportunity** is the sum of channel whitespace (20), conversion gap (20), timing (20), content-mix whitespace (20), and customer-value economics (20). **Response** is the sum of published contact route (20), named decision-maker access (20), strength of the specific observation (20), current trigger (20), and ease of proposing a bounded pilot (20).
- Outreach remains manual. Each draft below is a starting point and should be reviewed immediately before sending because offers and website conditions can change.

# Que Media hospitality and experience leads

Research captured July 20, 2026 and extended July 22, 2026. This set contains eight real Ottawa-area businesses selected for Que Media's strengths in entertaining and educational short-form video. Contact details were taken from official business properties or named professional profiles. No private information, guessed email pattern, invented metric, or purchased-list field is included.

The companion file, `hospitality-leads.json`, is the machine-readable source of truth. These are outreach-preparation records only. Nothing has been sent.

## Scoring method

- Content Fit measures how naturally the business supports visual stories, recurring series, staff or founder participation, community relevance, and trackable business outcomes.
- Opportunity measures the strength of an observable content, conversion, information, or campaign gap.
- Response estimates the quality of the public contact path, timing, evidence strength, and likelihood that a precise manual message earns attention.
- Overall is the rounded composite: 40% Content Fit, 35% Opportunity, and 25% Response.
- Every score is an analyst judgment supported by the cited evidence. It is not a claim about revenue, ad spend, or actual response probability.
